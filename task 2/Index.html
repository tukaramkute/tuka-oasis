BIOGRAPHY


Sundar Pichai is an Indian American business executive who is the Chief Executive Officer (CEO) of Google. He was the former product Chief of Google.

Pichai Sundararajan also known as Sundar Pichai was born in Madurai, Tamil Nadu, India in a middle class Brahmin family.Sundar Pichai's mother Lakshmi was a stenographer and his father, Regunatha Pichai was an electrical engineer at GEC,the British conglomerate. His father also had a manufacturing plant that produced electrical components.

After his education, Sundar Pichai worked in Engineering and Product management at applied materials at McKinsey & Company. He joined Google in 2004. He led the product development including Google Chrome. In 2009, he gave the demonstration of Chrome OS. In 2013, Pichai added Android to the list of Google products that he oversees. He was also the director of Jive Software from 2011-2013. In 2015, he was selected as next CEO of Google.
